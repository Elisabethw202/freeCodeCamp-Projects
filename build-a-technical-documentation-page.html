Intro to Digital Art

In this introduction, we will discussing what digital art is. Digital art, is similar to traditional art, only using technology and programs to create illustrations.

Though this is not everyone's forte, some people prefer this method, especially in this day and age. Most companies these days, in order to compete with time, use this method to speed up production and customer requests.

The lists below will show you specfic examples for each section.

Tools Reccomended

The following tools are reccomended when starting in Digital Art, easily requirable from cheap to a more costly price.

It is recommended you purchase or have some sore of "Art Tablet", this will help you control and work better with the illustration or design, ensuring a feel of traditional art (Ex: Tablet, Pen, Stand, Laptop/PC, Usb/& HDMI connector.

This list lists the reccommended tools.

  • Tablet
  • Digital Pen
  • Tablet-Stand
  • Laptop/PC
  • USB Connector
  • HDMI Connector
